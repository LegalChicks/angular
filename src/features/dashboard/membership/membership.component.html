Frequently Asked Questions

How does the collective marketing work?

We consolidate eggs from all our members, giving us the volume to negotiate better deals with hotels, restaurants, and retailers. Profits are distributed transparently back to our member partners.

What is the "Guided Business Warranty"?

It's our promise to mentor you actively from setting up your farm until you complete your first profitable harvest. We provide the training, answer your questions, and connect you with experts. Your success is our success.

Can I upgrade my membership tier later?

Absolutely! We encourage growth. You can easily upgrade from a Starter Package to a Full Membership as your farm and business ambitions expand. Contact our support team to discuss the process.
